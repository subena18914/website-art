I am immensely grateful to know with confidence that I am doing exactly what I was meant to do in my own life. Painting, teaching and helping other aspiring artists, and making attempts to create something beautiful or interesting is my life's work. My current work is concerned with culturally-charged, manufactured objects nearing obsolescence, their adjoining environments and the allegorical associations that are revealed through their traces and memories.

When creating an art, I try to respond to aspects of nature such as work of lights in the natural surroundings and also on objects, as Light gives life. This bridge between shape and the light can effectively erase the transition point between reality and illusion. The art thus becomes integrated into the real world of architecture and lends credibility to the illusion of the painting. Together the sum is greater than its parts.

Still I am learning and practicing to create a piece that really gives joy to others and to me. In this journey your inspiration and recommendations give me strength to concentrate on meaningful creation.

About Me

Name:Anwar-ul-Alam

Age:40

Location:Dhaka / Bangladesh

Anwar's artistic ability became evident in his youth, and by age 10, he was awarded in many national cultural competitions. Anwar was clearly a talented artist, and by age 14 he was selling drawings that were featured at his uncle's shop in Dhaka. Anwar is a Master of Science in Applied Physics and Electronics and he has got no formal education in fine arts, it is the talents inside him that has been driving out all creations from him. Due to his father’s govt. service in many districts Anwar’s family has to travel from place to place in years. He has captured beauty of nature and dance of lights on them in his memory while he was in country side. Anwar Sohel’s peaceful and contemplative oil paintings capture the spirit of the landscape. He prefers unscathed pristine scenes of nature, but also delights in river/canal scenes that show man living in harmony with nature.

Presently, Anwar is working with Oil and Acrylic colors, some of his artworks are also with water color. He is aiming for an exhibition soon to expose his art works to the out world.
